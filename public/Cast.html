Mitchell Hope

Plays the character of Ben. He is an Australian actor, starring in multiple films such as Love You Like that, Never Tear Us Apart, and more.

Cameron Boyce

Played the character of Carlos. Beginning his career as a child actor, he has appeared in multiple shows like Grown Ups and Jessie.

RIP May 28, 1999 - July 6, 2019

Dove Cameron

Plays the roles of Mal. She rose to fame for her dual role in the Disney Channel's comedy series, Liv and Maddie. Now, she is a also a singer and has starred in many more films.

Booboo Stewart

Plays the character of Jay. Besides his Descendants role, he is also well known for starring in The Twilight Saga, X-Men: Days of Future Past, and more.

Sofia Carson

Plays the role of Evie. Beyond acting, she is also an artist in which she has released many covers and singles. Furthermore, she has starred in more films such as My Oxford Year and Purple Hearts.
