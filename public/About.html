About Lia

Eiliyah Chryselle M. Beltran, also known as Lia, has been a fan of Descendants and their song “If Only” since 2018. She continues to regularly rewatch the movies and listen to their songs. Out of all the Descendants films, the first movie has always been her favorite, which is why it was chosen as the focus of this website.

The purpose of the website is to make other fans happy and to encourage those who haven’t watched the movie yet to explore it and learn more about it through the site.

About Carlo

Carlito Miguel Sia, nicknamed Carlo, is a student from PSHS-MC 9-Beryllium.

He has been an avid watcher of the Descendants series since 2017, following its development over time and enjoying its story and music. He shares the same favorites about the series as Lia, with "If Only" as his favorite song and the first movie as his favorite part as well.

Our website introduces the different cast members and characters, as well as a brief summary of the story of the first movie to introduce new fans or bring a feeling of nostalgia to old ones. Here, we also included some of our other favorite songs and our favorite lines in them.
