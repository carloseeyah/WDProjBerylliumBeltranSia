Ben

Ben, the prince of Auradon is the next heir to the throne as king. He plays as the love interest of Mal, testing her wits and heart for love and eventually looking to marry her.

Carlos

The son of Cruella De Vil. Initially having an irrational fear of canine, Carlos' character progresses and overcomes his fear with a new dog bestfriend, Dude.

Mal

The main protagonist of the show and the daughter of Malificent. She serves as the team's leader, initiating the plans and execution for their plan to steal the Fairy Godmother Wand.

Jay

The son of Jafar and the brawns and charm of the team. He is handsome, sneaky, mischievous, and becomes known for his athleticism as a star player on the tourney team in Auradon Prep.

Evie

The daughter of the evil queen. The beauty and brains of the team. With her mother's Magic Mirror, she uses it to help her and her team carry out their parents' evil plan.
